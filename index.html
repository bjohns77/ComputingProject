    Analysis of Arrests and Crime Victims in Los Angeles from 2020 to Present

    Overview

    This website provides a comprehensive analysis of the frequency of individuals arrested and crime victims in Los Angeles. The visualizations are constructed using two datasets from the Los Angeles Police Department Open Data Portal. The arrests dataset details over 280,000 arrests and the crime incident dataset contains over 932,000 crimes. Crimes and arrests in Los Angeles are investigated in terms of area, age, gender, race, type, and year.

    The goal of this website is to provide a greater understanding of arrest and crime frequency in Los Angeles, while highlighting potential bias in policing practices. Through a thorough investigation of crime and arrest data in the past few years, Los Angeles can work toward creating a more safe, just society.
